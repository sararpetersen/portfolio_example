There's been a little bit of a gap since my last entry here, and honestly, it wasn't for lack of things happening. Quite the opposite, actually. I just kept telling myself I'd write about it once things had settled down a bit, but that moment never really came. So here's the update I've been putting off, for the past two weeks.

Part of that gap was my 2nd semester exam, which I luckily ended up passing with a 7, plus prepping for the actual official start of my internship— both of which ate up more of my time than I expected. On top of that, I also finally got around to something I'd been putting off for even longer than this post: posting on the portfolio's social platforms instead of just having the accounts sit there.

Since I figured more people might actually visit the site now that I'm posting on social media, I also took the time to fix a bunch of small layout and accessibility issues that had been bothering me. It didn't help that one of my newest vibe coding projects turned out to be far from "fully responsive", which was reason enough to go through the entire portfolio and check tablet and mobile more thoroughly. Nothing flashy, just a more solid foundation underneath everything else I've built so far.
