These last few weeks have been somewhat different from my usual routine, which is also why I’ve been a bit absent recently. Nonetheless, it has been a generally positive change of pace.

Earlier this month, I attended my first few Folkæt meetings to stay informed about the project and better understand the upcoming steps, some of which I covered in another blog post. These were mainly just regular weekly check-ins, held every Monday and Saturday, but they still made things feel more organised before the weekend started.

Another big thing that has happened over the past few weeks is that I finally started medication for my ADD. I’m still adjusting, but it has already helped me far more than I expected.
At the same time, last week from Thursday to late Sunday evening, I was over visiting my boyfriend for his 26th birthday and spending some quality time with him, so I wasn’t in my usual home environment for a few days. Even though it was a break from my normal routine, it was still nice to slow down a bit and just step away from everything for a moment.

Overall, it was nice to have a slower weekend while still remaining engaged. It allowed me to relax a bit and return feeling rejuvenated and prepared for the week ahead.
